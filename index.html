Data Processing & Feature Engineering

AI bots use machine learning techniques to process vast amounts of data and identify key indicators or patterns that might suggest a buying or selling opportunity. Technical Indicators: These can include moving averages, RSI (Relative Strength Index), MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence), Bollinger Bands, etc. Sentiment Analysis: Some bots also use natural language processing (NLP) to analyze news or social media sentiment around certain assets.

Model Training & Backtesting

Supervised Learning: In some cases, bots are trained on labeled datasets, where they learn from past trades and try to predict future prices. Reinforcement Learning: In other cases, the bot learns by trial and error, receiving rewards for profitable trades and penalties for losses. Backtesting: Before deploying a bot, it is tested on historical data to see how it would have performed in past markets. This helps fine-tune its algorithms
